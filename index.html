Master Tsumori Hayenami

A Lifetime of Craftsmanship

Tsumori Hayenami (津守 颯波), now a revered 65-year-old sushi master, was born in a small, rural village nestled in the coastal mountains of Japan. His journey into the world of sushi began at the age of 16, when he left his hometown to apprentice under a skilled sushi chef in the bustling city of Osaka. Inspired by the freshness of the sea and the simplicity of life back home, Hayenami immersed himself in the art of sushi, dedicating himself to perfecting the delicate balance of flavor, texture, and presentation.
